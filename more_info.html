About covid 19

Covid 19, or its real name Sars-Cov-2 is a virus from the Corona Viruses family and a close member of the Sars family. The Covid 19 virus affects mainly the lungs and causes symptoms similar to the common cold like Runny nose, cough, and sore throat. The coronavirus can cause severe disease and, in some cases, death. Currently, Omicron is the newest variant of the coronavirus and, it is less invasive than its older brother Delta variant. However, Omicron can cause severe disease. Also, statistically, every variant of the virus is less invasive but more infectious. In conclusion, the coronavirus has changed our lives and will forever.
